Chest Pain Categorization (History)

  • Highly Suspicious (2 points): Central pressure, radiation, exertional, nitro relief, with symptoms like dyspnea or nausea.
  • Moderately Suspicious (1 point): Atypical symptoms with some anginal hints.
  • Slightly Suspicious (0 points): Pleuritic, positional, reproducible, or GERD/anxiety-related pain.
